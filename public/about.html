    Who are We?


    Prayaas Legal is a full-service Law Firm based in New Delhi. Prayaas Legal’s strength lies in the quality of its lawyers and solution-oriented approach.

    Our comprehensive domain knowledge and depth of experience across sectors empowers us to consider strategic concerns while crafting accurate, bespoke solutions for our clients.
